Make a perfect cup of tea with just a few pages!

Ingredients

1 cup water
1 tea bag
1 tea spoon sugar (optional)
1/2 cup milk (optional)
1 slice of lemon(optional)

Instructions

1. Boil Water
2. Place tea bag in a cup
3. Pour hot water over the tea.
4. Steep for3-5 minutes
5. Add sugar or honey if you like.
6. Add milk if you prefer.
7. Add lemon if desired.
8. Enjoy your tea!
